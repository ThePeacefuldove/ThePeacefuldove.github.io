OUR STORY

At Ground Burger Bar, we're focused on keeping it in the neighbourhood. We source locally whenever possible, featuring Penokean Hills Farm (Bruce Mines) Beef, Headwater Farms (Uxbridge) Wagyu, Thunder Ridge Farm (Uxbridge) Bison. Our draft beers not only highlight some of the best local microbreweries, but some of the best in the world. Located in the heart of Newmarket, just off Main Street, we're never far from where you are. Join us for a burger and beer today!
