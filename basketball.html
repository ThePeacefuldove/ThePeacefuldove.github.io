What is basketball

A basketball is a round, inflated ball used in the team sport of the same name, where the objective is to score points by shooting the ball through a hoop while playing on a rectangular court. Invented by James Naismith in 1891

5 standard positions

  1. Point Guard(PG)
  2. Shooting Guard(SG)
  3. Small Foward(SF)
  4. Power Foward(PF)
  5. Center(C)
